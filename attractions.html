ATTRACTIONS

Rides

Fall Into Hades

This drop tower climbs high into the air over 400 feet. But it does not stop here... Before it drops, its seats pivot 90 degrees so that passengers face the ground as they freefall at a furious 60 mph.

Styx River Cruise

This rollercoaster is identified as a psychological thrill ride. It aims to play mind games with its riders. This attraction has it all: Hermes will lead the passengers to Charon as they gaze into the depths of the darkest river.

Realm of Darkness

A harrowing rollercoaster, that reaches 4.5 G, a height of 203 feet, and speeds exceeding 120 kmph. And there are seven coveted moments of weightlessness when your stomach just floatsâ€¦and floats again.

Hades' Eye

To say this pendulum thrill ride is an adrenaline rush is an understatement. Hades' Eye takes its passengers nearly vertical at the height of each swinging arc, hits a top speed of 70 kmph, and delivers potent, tummy-tickling, negative G-forces.

Tartarus

A deep abyss used as a dungeon of torment and suffering for the wicked. Do you think you can escape? Try your luck at the Tartarus and test your wits while the clock ticks.

Cerberus

Take your children to this not-so-sweet merry-go-round and have them ride the guardians of the underworld. Safe for children of all ages - not counting the nightmares, of course.

Asterion's Maze

The legend goes that the minotaur was recruited by Hades to prevent his son, Zagreus, from escaping the Underworld. Try to find your way inside the maze before the minotaur catches you!

Food Court

Mourning Fields

The Mourning Fields was a section of the underworld reserved for souls who wasted their lives on unrequited love. Bring your date and draw your fate. This restaurant lets you take a break from all the horror. You'll listen to soothing romantic songs while you enjoy the best of the greek cuisine.

Flegetonte

Plato described the Flagetonte river as "a stream of fire, which coils round the earth and flows into the depths of Tartarus". Beware with the hot cheese in this italian pizzaria!

Elysium

Rest your soul at this fine dining restaurant at the end of Hades! Find the best of contemporary cuisine here with some celestial, hand-selected wines.
